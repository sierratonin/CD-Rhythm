Because The Internet - Childish Gambino


Childish Gambino's second album Because The Internet is very different from his first, Camp. While in some ways this album shows his variability with some extremely catchy and interesting tracks, the rest is a bit rough trying to get through. This album was a risk, and at some points it was worth it, but at others it was not.

Nectar - Joji


Joji's albums continue to get better and better. Nectar is a beautiful collection of Lo-Fi beats, astonishing vocals, and emotional 3 am potentially crying on the floor vibes.

Planet Her - Doja Cat


This album is extremely versatile. While on the surface Doja Cat gives off a R&B / pop / rap persona, there is so much more going on in this album. There is inspiration in this album ranging from reggaeton to hyperpop, and plenty in between. Doja's ability to transition between clever lyricism in her raps to heartfelt ballads is impeccable.
