GENRES

Pop


Pop music is very broad and hard to pinpoint, because it generally encompasses all mainstream music, or 'pop'ular music. Other genres are often represented in pop music, and the most popular genres shape what pop music is at any particular time. To be a bit more specific, pop music generally includes a catchy melody and lyrics.

TOP POP ARTISTS

Ariana Grande
Doja Cat
Taylor Swift

Rap


Rap, sometimes referred to as hip-hop music, is a genre where lyrics are performed in a way that is closer to speaking than singing. Oftentimes rap can be characterized by complex and meaningful lyrics and rhymes.

TOP RAP ARTISTS

Childish Gambino
Eminem
Kendrick Lamar

R&B


R&B music, or rhythm and blues, is a genre of music derived from gospel, jazz, folk, and blues, which began recieving popularity around the same time and rock 'n' roll. R&B is typically defined by its use of keyboards, synthesizers, strong bass lines, and looped drum beats.

TOP R&B ARTISTS

Beyonce
SZA
The Weeknd
