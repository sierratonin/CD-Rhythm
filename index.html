About CD-RHYTHM


Welcome to CD-RHYTHM! This website is a place for music lovers to explore new music, and for me to share my personal thoughts on music. I was inspired by some other incredible music reviewers such as HIVEMINDTV and TheNeedleDrop. Although I may not have the backing behind me to give incredibly meaningful reviews like them, I am excited to share some opinions and have fun.

My name is Sierra McKinley, and this website was created for my CIS 300: Web Design and Development class. Please enjoy looking around!

This site has many pages offering:

  • Music Reviews
  • Artist Profiles
  • Personalized Playlists
  • Genre Overviews
  • My Personal Favorites
